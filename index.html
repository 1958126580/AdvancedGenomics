AdvancedGenomics.jl is a high-performance package designed for modern quantitative genetics and breeding. It integrates state-of-the-art statistical methods with deep learning to solve complex genomic problems.

  • GWAS: Linear Mixed Models (FarmCPU, BLINK), Logistic Regression, Meta-Analysis.
  • Genomic Selection: GBLUP, Bayesian Alphabet (BayesA/B/C), Machine Learning.
  • Deep Learning: Genomic Transformers, Graph Neural Networks (GNN).
  • HPC: GPU Acceleration and Multi-threading.
